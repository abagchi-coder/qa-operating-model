# QA Operating Model

This repository defines an enterprise-scale Quality Assurance Operating Model designed to enable predictable, high-confidence software delivery across complex, multi-team environments.

## Objectives
- Establish clear quality ownership and accountability
- Enable shift-left and defect prevention
- Align QA strategy with Agile, SAFe, and DevOps delivery models
- Provide transparency through metrics and governance

## Scope
This model is applicable for:
- Large enterprises with multiple products and distributed teams
- Regulated and high-risk domains (Capital Markets, Healthcare, etc.)
- Organizations transitioning from project-based to product-based delivery

## Key Components
- Operating Model & Engagement
- Governance & Quality Gates
- Metrics & Business Impact
- Agile & SAFe Alignment
- Automation & AI Strategy
- Maturity Model & Roadmap

This is a reference model based on industry practices and leadership experience. It does not contain any proprietary or client-specific material.
