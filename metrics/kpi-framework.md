# Quality Metrics Framework

## Delivery Health
- Release predictability (% on-time)
- Defect leakage to production
- Reopen rate

## Product Quality
- Defect density
- Escaped defect severity
- Customer-reported issues

## Process Efficiency
- Test automation coverage
- Regression execution time
- Environment stability

## Business Impact
- Revenue impact of defects
- Cost of rework
- CSAT / NPS correlation
