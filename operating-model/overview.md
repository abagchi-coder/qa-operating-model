# Operating Model Overview

The QA Operating Model defines how quality is planned, governed, executed, and continuously improved across the organization.

## Principles
- Quality is owned by the entire product team
- QA acts as an enabler, not a gatekeeper
- Prevention over detection
- Automation-first and risk-based
- Data-driven decision making

## Core Capabilities
- Test Strategy & Planning
- Release Quality Assurance
- Automation Enablement
- Risk & Compliance
- Metrics & Insights
